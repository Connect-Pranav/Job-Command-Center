🤝 Negotiation Playbook

      

        

          
✅ Do This

          
    
            
  • Lead with ₹80L/month P&L ownership — sets scope
    • 
            
  • Mention IIM Kozhikode as future upside they're getting early
    • 
            
  • Quote CAC −25% project as ROI-proven thinking
    • 
            
  • Say: "I'm evaluating 25–32L range"
    • 
            
  • Always get the CTC breakup in writing before accepting
    • 
          

        

        

          
⚠ Avoid This

          
    
            
  • Don't anchor below 22L — undersells 9 years of P&L
    • 
            
  • Never justify with "I need" — use market data instead
    • 
            
  • Don't reveal current salary until you have an offer
    • 
            
  • Don't accept verbal offers without written breakup
    • 
            
  • Avoid desperation language even if timeline is tight
